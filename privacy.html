Last Updated — April 24, 2026

Lamrichter values your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website — plainly, without the fine print.

// Privacy Policy
I.
Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary for account creation and login:

  • Account Information — Username, email address, and password (stored securely)
  • Basic Technical Data — IP address and browser type for security purposes
II.
How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Allow you to log in securely
  • Protect against unauthorized access or misuse

We do not use your data for advertising or tracking.

III.
Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

We may only disclose information if required by law or to protect the integrity and security of our platform.

IV.
Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data:

  • Passwords are encrypted and hashed
  • Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel

However, no online system is completely secure. We encourage responsible account management.

V.
Data Retention

We keep your account information for as long as your account exists and is in use.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the address below.

VI.
Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your account

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

VII.
Children's Privacy

Lamrichter is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it promptly.

VIII.
Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements.

Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

IX.
Contact Us
