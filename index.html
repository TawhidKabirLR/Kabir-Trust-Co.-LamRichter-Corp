// About Lam Richter

Real estate that
performs.

Lam Richter Corp. was founded to bring institutional discipline to real estate investment. We identify, acquire, and actively manage properties across residential, commercial, and mixed-use asset classes — combining rigorous underwriting with hands-on asset management to generate durable returns.

In partnership with Kabir Trust & Co., we serve private wealth clients and family offices seeking direct real estate exposure alongside comprehensive wealth management — all under one roof.

Our Strength
Acquisition Discipline

We source, underwrite, and close transactions with institutional precision — rigorous due diligence, disciplined pricing, and relentless deal focus.

Our Reach
Multi-Market Presence

Active across 18 metropolitan markets, Lam Richter identifies opportunities before they become consensus — giving clients access to deals the broader market hasn't priced yet.

Our Edge
Active Management

We don't just buy and hold — we actively manage every asset to maximize NOI, occupancy, and long-term appreciation across the full holding period.
