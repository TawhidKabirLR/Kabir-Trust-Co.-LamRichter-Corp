// About Lam Richter

Real estate that
performs.

Lam Richter Corp. was founded to bring institutional discipline to real estate investment. We identify, acquire, and actively manage properties across residential, commercial, and mixed-use asset classes — combining rigorous underwriting with hands-on asset management to generate durable returns.

Our Strength

We source, underwrite, and close transactions with institutional precision — rigorous due diligence, disciplined pricing, and relentless deal focus.

Our Reach

We don't just buy and hold — we actively manage every asset to maximize NOI, occupancy, and long-term appreciation.
